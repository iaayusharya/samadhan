Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report an issue?

You can report an issue by filling out the form in the "Report Issue" section. Provide your name, email, issue description, and select the concerned department.

How long does it take to resolve an issue?

Issue resolution time depends on the complexity of the problem and the department involved. We aim to resolve issues as quickly as possible.

Can I track the status of my issue?

Currently, we do not have a tracking system, but we are working on adding this feature soon.
